The difference between lossy and lossless compression on images

Lossy and Lossless are compresion terms that describe whether
or not, when an image is compressed, if all original data can
be recovered when the file is uncompressed.[1]

Why is it important for web designers to compress image files?

There are many reasons why web creators
should compress their files,here is a list of some:

  • Bigs graphics will make your page load
    slowly. This can make visitors uninterested
    and they may leave your website.[2]
  • By using large files, you effectively remove
    yourself from the market of people that do not
    have broadband internet (which is most users), as
    they cannot load files that have a high file size.[2]

    • The effects of lossy and lossless compression

    The effects of lossy compression is to reduce the file
    size by removing data that isn't as important as the rest
    of the data, and the effect of lossles compression is to
    reduce the size of the file without have a noticeable
    diffecerence on the image.
